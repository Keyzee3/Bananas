Directions

Step One

Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan. Ingredients for banana bread including mashed banana butter flour brown sugar egg mixture salt and baking soda

Step Two

Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl. Beat brown sugar and butter with an electric mixer in a separate large bowl until smooth. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended. Stir banana mixture into flour mixture until just combined. Banana bread batter being mixed in a glass bowl with a spatula

Step Three

Pour batter into the prepared loaf pan. Batter spread evenly in a rectangular baking pan ready to be baked into banana bread

Step Four

Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 60 minutes. Freshly baked banana bread in a rectangular pan with a toothpick and a checkered towel beside it

Step Five

Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack to cool completely. Loaf of banana bread on a cooling rack

Step Six

Enjoy!
